DevOps Setup

DevOps is the backbone of modern engineering. MEHTALOGY LABS helps organizations design, implement, and optimize DevOps workflows including CI/CD pipelines, GitOps automation, infrastructure-as-code, and complete observability stacks. From multi-environment deployment automation to cost tracking and security scanning, we empower your engineering teams to ship faster and more reliably.

How This Service Helps

A strong DevOps foundation improves deployment speed, reduces outages, and enhances developer productivity across teams.

Business Goals

  • Accelerate release cycles and reduce lead time
  • Increase deployment reliability through automation
  • Improve visibility with logs, metrics and traces
  • Shift-left security through DevSecOps

Key Capabilities & Features

  • Automated CI/CD (GitHub Actions, GitLab, Azure DevOps)
  • GitOps platform design (ArgoCD, Flux)
  • Infrastructure-as-Code (Terraform, Pulumi, Bicep)
  • Monitoring and observability stacks (Prometheus, Grafana, Loki)
  • DevSecOps: SAST/DAST scanning, SBOM, vulnerability management
