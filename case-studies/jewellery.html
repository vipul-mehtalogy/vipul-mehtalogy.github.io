Jewellery Product Catalog & Real-time Pricing Transformation

Jewellery retailers face challenges unique to the industry: rapidly fluctuating prices, multi-variant products, inventory shrinkage, and operational inefficiency. We helped Sapna Shri Jewellers digitize their entire sales and product experience with a smart real-time catalogue powered by pricing engines, smart automation and inventory control.

The Challenge

  • Pricing changes multiple times a day
  • No central catalog for products and variants
  • Product listing changes were manual & slow
  • Customer trust depends heavily on transparency
  • High operational load and dependency on staff
  • Theft risk and missing item tracking was manual

Our Solution

We built a hybrid platform that provides a real-time connected product catalog, automated pricing engine, and a digital inventory intelligence layer.

Automated Dynamic Product Catalog

Products sync automatically from inventory and pricing logic. Each product receives SEO-ready pages, images, and descriptions.

Real-Time Pricing Engine

Gold and diamond prices auto-update across all products with automated recalculation. No human error, no downtime.

Inventory & Stock Tracking

Monitor live stock, track quantity changes, and control item availability across showroom and website.

Anti-Theft Item Tracking

Smart software keeps a watch on boxes presented to customers. Reduces inventory shrinkage and improves security.

Unique Item Identification

Each jewellery item receives a unique digital ID for tracking, audit, reordering, history and authentication.

Sale & Billing Integration

Sold items auto-remove from website & catalogue instantly, ensuring real-time stock accuracy.

Push Notifications

Price drop alerts, offers, festival campaigns & new arrivals are broadcast directly to customer browsers & mobile.

Daily Reports & Analytics

Track daily sales, view product demand, performance and generate operational insights automatically.
